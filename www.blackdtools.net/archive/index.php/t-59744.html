Yet another basic script for people coming out of rook, this one is a little more difficult then the Thais Trolls because you come across some rotworms and the swamp trolls deal consistent poison damage, so make sure again enable your auto-eat food on Runemaker, Equip a decent enough set to handle Rotworms/Swamp trolls and engage your auto-heals and bring a few extra mana potions incase of PKs. That being said, this is a much better money maker then the Thais Trolls, due to being able to collect Medicine pouches while hunting, and the chance to come across feverish citizens and loot a few add-on pieces. Make changes where required if you wish to deposit after more then 4 Medicine pouches or more then 1000 gold. Again, this is basic but works nonetheless, pre-equip your characters because there is no re-fillers only a depositer. This is for learning purposes and I hope more people start to contribute to our community.

Required; Lvl ~10 Wound Cleansing/Light Healing, 10-20 Potions and decent equipment to deal with the Rotworms.

Note: Start this script in Venores East depot, where the banker is located and Shovel & Rope is required.

Features:


*** FEATURES ***
* Versatile healer with unlimited amount of priorities
* Simple and powerful hotkey system
* A simple lighthack
* A simple antiparalyze


*** HELP ***

* Healer
--- make sure you put the lowest hp first
--- otherwise it might not work as expected
--- you can sort the list by clicking the 3 top cells

* Hotkeys (note: all hotkeys are not case-sensitive):
toggle arg1
--- arg1 = hotkeys / tibiawindow (shows/hides Tibia)
ss / screenshot / screenshoot
--- takes a screenshot
--- settings can be found in settings.ini
F1 - F12
--- can also be used with shift or ctrl
--- example: shift+f2
Up / Right / Down / Left
--- walks in given direction

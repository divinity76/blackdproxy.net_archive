I am receiving messages of people that can't connect to Tibia with blackd proxy this days.
The problem is one of the tibia login servers ( login01.tibia.com ) is bugged and apparently working but not really working.

To solve this you should go to Proxy menu and select other server:

For example: login02.tibia.com

Then try login in Tibia again.

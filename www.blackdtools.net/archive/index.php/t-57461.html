This Venore Swamp Troll script does not kill ghouls or skeletons unless they're lured.
It also contains a Medicine Pouch Exchanger, if you have the setting fastExiva _exchangemedicine set to true (Default mode is "false", off) it will after depositing and dropping off other loot and buying potions go to Ottokar and exchange and use the Belongings of a Deceased. It will then go to Venore big depot and deposit the medium to good stuff. And ofcourse rare, altough I miss Slug Drug ID. :mad:
These items are default to deposit:
- Bat Wing (always keeps because looted from bats in cave.
- Honeycomb
- Spider Fangs
- Bunch of Troll Hair
- Gold Coins
- Chicken Feather
- Turtle Shell
- Garlic Necklace
- Spider Silk
- White Pearl
- Piggy Bank
- Book of Prayers
- Iron Ore
- Flask of Warrior's Sweat
- Plague Bell
- Plague Mask
- Boots of Haste
- Slug Drug Don't know ID!

And to drop the following:
- Egg
- Broken Piggy Bank
- Dirty Cape
- Worn Leather Boots

It will otherwise just deposit Medicine Pouches in depot. Does not kill Cave Rats or Snakes, I made this script just to hunt for Medicine Pouches pretty much. :cool:

Enjoy!

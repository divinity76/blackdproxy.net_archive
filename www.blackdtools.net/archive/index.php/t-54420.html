Mintwallin script, recommanded for level 30 to 40. often empty. I'd recommand atleast a knight set with a decent shield. (Full crown set would be perfect)
I've tested it on my 26 knight (49/44 -higher skills is means less waste- with full crown set & wyvern fang) and it works just fine with little waste.

got an average of 18-21k exp/hour alone.

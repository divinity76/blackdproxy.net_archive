Aimbot
Easiest way to cast sd or UE combo, available on version 1.5.
The leader just need to say target name or spell.

http://i.imgur.com/DVrBvjG.png

http://i.imgur.com/xG8tbRV.png
Picture from version 1.5

Leader: The name who will lead combo, its case-sensitive.

Aim type: Casts rune combo when Leader say target's name. It accepts "sd", "hmm", "explo" and "icicle".
sd is default.

Spell combo: Its the spell your character will execute when Leader say something determinated on Spell sync.

Sync combo: When Leader say <Sync combo> bot will execute Spell combo

Example:


Spell combo: exevo gran mas frigo
Sync combo: good bye
When Leader say "good bye", you cast "exevo gran mas frigo".

Spell combo: exevo gran mas flam
Sync combo: exevo gran mas flam
When Leader say "exevo gran mas flam" you cast "exevo gran mas flam". Its UE combo.


Execute Aim type: enable to cast rune combo (SD) when Leader say target's name.

Active UE combo: enable to cast what is writen on "Spell combo" when Leader say what is in "Sync Combo".

Lock on leader's target: set meele attack to target when Leader say target's name.

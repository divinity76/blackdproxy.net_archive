If you use my method posted here for restocking spears:
http://www.blackdtools.net/showthread.php?57511-HOW-TO-Spear-Refiller
It's extremely easy, just changing the ID, weight and price.

If the script does not use any configuration but have hardcoded values, you need to find the correct numbers and edit them.
CD 0C => D2 1C
Weight 20 => 25
Price 9 => 15
You shouldn't try to do that unless you know how numericalexp works though.

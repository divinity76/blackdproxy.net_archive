in settings.ini , after you reload blackd proxy, you will find this under [Cavebot] section :

EXORIVIS_COST=20
EXORIVIS_SPELL=exori vis

and then you can modify that. It is important to have Blackd Proxy closed while changing the .ini

---------

Updated since 9.55 . Now you can also use setExoriMort . You can also play with 2 different settings in settings.ini :

EXORIVIS_COST=20
EXORIVIS_SPELL=exori vis

EXORIMORT_COST=20
EXORIMORT_SPELL=exori mort

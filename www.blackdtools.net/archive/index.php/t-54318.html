Hello.

I made two scripts for lov lvls pally , the first one , min 10 lvl - 3-4k/h , the second 5-6k on 14-17 lvl.

You should bring some health potions , remember set autorefill on trainer !

Both got spear buyer and depositor .

First .


#By Wiotki
setLootOn
setLoot D7 0B
setChaoticMovesOFF
setMeleeKill Orc
setPriority Orc Spearman:+1
setMeleeKill Orc Spearman
setMeleeKill Orc Warrior
#exp
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32332,32189,10
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32332,32198,10
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32322,32169,10
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32312,32166,10
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32332,32152,10
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#5) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32332,32189,10
gotoScriptLine 0
:spear
move 32332,32190,9
move 32356,32183,9
move 32356,32174,9
move 32356,32172,8
move 32356,32169,7
move 32344,32228,7
sayMessage hi
waitX 2
sayInTrade deposit all
waitX 2
sayInTrade yes
waitX 2
fastExiva _spearneed = $numericalexp:{$mycap$}/2000$
fastExiva _spearcount = $numericalexp:{$_spearneed$}-1$
fastExiva _spearcost = $numericalexp:{$_spearcount$}*9$
sayInTrade withdraw $_spearcost$
waitX 2
sayInTrade yes
move 32349,32246,7
move 32350,32245,6
move 32343,32246,6
sayInTrade Hello
waitX 2
sayInTrade trade
waitX 2
fastExiva > 07 00 7A CD 0C 00 $numbertohex2:{$_spearcount$}$ 00
waitX 2
move 32350,32245,6
move 32350,32247,7
move 32368,32214,7
move 32359,32172,7
move 32357,32170,7
move 32356,32171,8
move 32356,32174,9
move 32333,32189,9
gotoScriptLine 0

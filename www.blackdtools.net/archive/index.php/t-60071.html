Well, I always use HP&MANA section. Click on it and configure it appropriately for your desirable vlaues.

http://i.imgur.com/KRsjAFt.png

http://i.imgur.com/dpOg2gW.png

1.) Yellow square = Choose your character's name.
2.) Red square = Set % for HP and MANA or only for HP/MANA. As you wish.
3.) Green square = Choose the option of healing/refilling. For your case obviously 'Heal with health potion'.
4.) Orange square = Click on 'ADD AS NEW SETTING' which self explanatory ads your choice as a new setting.
5.) White square = Click on 'SAVE ON HD' which will save it on hard drive, so everytime you open a bot you don't have to do all the healing settings again.

Ok, this seems to be a common question lately, so I am sharing the answer with everybody to avoid writing the same a lot of times.

Question: "Do you recommend me any account seller?"

My answer:

Sorry, I don't know about any seller who returns you the money if your account gets hacked. Sooner or later everybody gets hacked 9 of 10 times, for one reason or other. That is why I don't support any account seller. And I can't recommend you any.

Some facts to keep in mind:
- The original owner of the account can ALWAYS recover the account.
- If you buy an account then you are NOT the original owner of that account.
- 9 of 10 persons in Tibia are evil persons that will scam anyone whenever there is any chance to do it.
- The account sellers usually resell the accounts of unknown original owners and they reject any responsability over the actions of the original owner.


At the end you (almost) always lose:
- money
- account
- time invested in that account
- any item bought to chars of that account.

Do you really want to take the big risk? It is up to you.


Question: Then, What should I do?

My answer:

Make your own account from scratch, and buy gold to level it much faster. That is the only 100% safe method to get a high level account.

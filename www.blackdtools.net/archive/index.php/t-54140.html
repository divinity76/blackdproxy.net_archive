Do you mean the ramps at depot of Port Hope?

It seems to be a new kind of ramp. It will be added in 19.1 .
Until that you can just add the tile id B7 07 manually to AditionalStairsToUpFloor in config871\config.ini so you will now read this:

AditionalStairsToUpFloor=AC 07,AE 07,AA 07,94 08,96 08,90 08,92 08,03 1B,FF 1A,FD 1A,78 1D,76 1D,58 03,57 03,B7 07


Then just save that config.ini and reload Blackd Proxy. Then that ramp should work for cavebot now.

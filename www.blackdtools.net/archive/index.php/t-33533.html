My Exori strike conditionals work perfectly with wands and rods. They don't waste mana casting the spell when monster is already dead. You can kill the monster with one shot of wand and one exori, which was impossible with SetSpellKill function.

ATTENTION: It works only if you delete SetSpellKill commands for the same monsters you want to kill with conditional Exori.


The Cavebot SetSpellKill spams Exori Vis like crazy and your mage doesn't shoot with wand/rod and it casts 1 more spell when monster is already dead. If you kill your monsters with 1-2 Exori, THIS WILL SAVE YOU 20-40% OF THE HUNTING COST

You stop casting Exori when the monster is below X hp. This way you will finish them off with your wand and NEVER waste a spell again.

You can optimize the amount of Exori Vis/Frigo/Terra/Mort you use on the monsters. You can kill monsters with 1 wand shot and 1 Exori, or 2 wand shots and 1 Exori. This is extremely cheaper than traditional SetSpellKill.

The RED VALUE is the %hp below which you want to stop casting Exori spell. Change it depending on the strength of your wand and monster's hp.

The Blue Text is the spell you want to attack the monsters with.

The Green Text are names of monsters you want to attack with exori. You can copy this line as many times as needed, changing just the names of monsters.

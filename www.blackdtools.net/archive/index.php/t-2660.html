Hello, this will be a short tutorial about ventrilo. The SS's will be taken from VentriloMix.

The client i use can be DL'd from here (http://www.strawberrypancakes.org/VentriloMix.zip) [note by blackd: do not trust blindy on executables posted by users. They can be infected with keylogers. In this case I strongly recommend you search it i google instead downloading from this link]

so,

1th you download ventrilo and install it from there.
After you install it and open it you shud get this window on your scren.
http://img115.imageshack.us/img115/3546/ventmy7.jpg

After that press Ventrilo 2.1 and you'll get this window up

http://img442.imageshack.us/img442/5459/ventwg6.jpg

Press No and you'll get this up

http://img117.imageshack.us/img117/5346/ventiv5.jpg

Here you 1th of all press the -> and enter your name (eg. Sune^) and then just leave everything els and press Ok.

Now press the 2nd -> and press New then enter following
Name: Just w/e you want. (eg. BlackD)
Hostname or IP: Ip
Port Number: port
Password: pass
Default Channel: Leave this.

Now press Connect and your logged on.

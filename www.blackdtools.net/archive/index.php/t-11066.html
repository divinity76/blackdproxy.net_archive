Hiya my first tut, hope you like it and leave a comment ;).

One:

_/ Never go to the same cave or hunt spot.

I can hear you thinking, yes a good script :), I leave my bot here for the rest of the week!
Bad idea, if people see you more then once in the cave, or walking the same line all along, they start to ask questions, and you being there all the times piises them off, so they go cry at a gm.

Two:

_/ Time period.

Do not become 'greedy' with you blackd bot, leaving the bot running for a full night or longer then ~12-20 hours isnt a good plan.
It will result in traps, getting pk'd or discovered by a GM.

Three:

_/ Avoid the 'rush hours'.

at around 18.00 am entire 'tibian' europe will be playing, so better not to bot in a popular cave then, instead leave the bot running in the morning or afternoon, and do something else in evenings ;)
(ONLY FOR EUROPE TIME ZONES)

Four:

_/ Be Prepared.

Kinda logical, but not harmfull to mention, always make an alarm go off when you're low on HP, gm approciates, other player attacks you etc..

Hopefully it was helpfull, and GL with it

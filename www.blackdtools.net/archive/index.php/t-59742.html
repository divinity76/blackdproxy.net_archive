I got very annoyed of typing in conditionals each and every time setting up my bots back in the day, I finally came up with the idea of creating blank notepads in the conditions folder labeled, Knight, Paladin, Mage so whenever I needed to play a different class quickly I can just load on auto-heals auto-drink manas and go. So, I have simplified it for you guys who don't really know how to use auto-heals w/ ( Conditional events ).


In the BlackD Proxy folder, there is a folder called "conds" input these three .txt files into the folder, either re-start BlackD and or hit the (<-Reload) button in the conditional screen.

Voila, you now should be auto-healing and auto-drinking manas when low depending on what class you chose to load! These are also changeable right in the conditional menu screen by simply changing the number to your liking and hit "Modify selected condition".

Hope this helped anyone,

First of all, those are some very simple bot scripts, no one have deposit system so they're just for looting
and leveling up.

The first: Troll Cave

-Low lvl char (suggested 8+)
-Location: Noth from Thais.
-Start in the first lvl of the cave, where the snakes are.
-Prepare to face: troll, bug, bat, poison spider, snake, cave rat.


The second: Folda Rots Cave

-Low lvl char (suggested 14+)
-Location: Folda, cave lvl 9, 1 lvl before water elemental respawn.
-Start just when coming down from the ladder.
Prepare to face: rotworm, frost troll, bug, poison spider, slime, minotaur, minotaur archer.


The third: Ghouls zone at Kazzordoon mines.

-Meduim lvl char (suggested 24+)
-Location: Ghouls respawn zone in Kazzordoon mines. Not in the lvl where the bonelords are.
-Start in the cave that leads to the respawn of 1 dwarf guard and some dwarf soldier.
-Prepare to face: ghoul, skeleton, cave rat, dwarf soldier, only one dwarf guard (dwarfs are to get food, mushrooms)

I won't go into detail on how to enter the Vault because that's available from TibiaWikia. You do however need to get the keys, copper key 4502 to enter and exit easily, also key 4501 in your ammoslot to open one of the doors.
I don't know why, but it didn't work to open the door with the key when using a hotkey to use it.
Anyways, you probarly want to be in the lvl 30-40 range and a knight to use this script. It kills Minotaurs/Guards/Archers/Mages and Beholders and some odd small mobs between the major spawns. Neither the xp or the loot is that great but I don't think there's alot of people there.

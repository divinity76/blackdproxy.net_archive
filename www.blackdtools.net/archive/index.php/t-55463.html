You could use the trainer to put the food on ammo slot and then drop it with a simple conditional event.

exiva > 78 FF FF 0X 00 00 $hex-equiped-item:0X$ 00 XX XX YY YY ZZ CC
0X is the slot to move from and X, Y and Z's are the position to drop at. CC is the amount.

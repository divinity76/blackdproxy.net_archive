Hi guys,

I just made my first script for my lvl 13 paladin. I recently started playing Tibia again with some friends and I started to use the Blackdtools Proxy bot. Since my char is a paladin many scripts don't work for me since they don't replenish my spears. I tried some scripts that I found on this forum, but they never satisfied my needs. So I decided to make my own 100% AFK paladin script. After I read some tutorials I started to modify an existing Edron Rotworm script that I found to be the best so far (Edron Rotworms Seller and Depositer (http://www.blackdtools.net/showthread.php?47576-Edron-Rotworms-%28Seller-and-Depositer%29)). However it kept bugging with the modifications and it wasn't 100% AFK proof since I kept dying because I ran out of spears. So I made a new script completely written by myself that would fulfill my needs. I only copied most of the coordinates from the previously mentioned script which had been copied from an unknown script (so no credits). The result was a 100% AFK bot that I could successfully use.

This script makes sure you carry 15 spears and 10 health potions with you to minimize the chance of dying by running out of spears or extreme health loss. This was done to make it 100% AFK proof. At every waypoint in the cave the script will check for the amount of equipped spears (3 or more), the amount of health potions in the inventory (2 or more), your leftover capacity (30/50 or more) and your leftover hit points (20% or more). When one of these things gets to low it will exit the cave after eliminating all creeps at the waypoint and start selling the loot. It sells your looted swords, maces and dirt (if present in inventory to limit wasted time by moving) while replenishing your spears and health potions if needed. At the Ivory towers it sells the dirt and the empty flasks and it will replenish your potions. Make sure to set your HP/Mana regulation by loading conditionals or using the 'HP & MANA' tool implemented in Blackdtools Proxy. You might need some testing done so you can optimize these settings. It will sell the loot, buy potions and spears and deposit all remaining gold before going back to the cave. Since auto eat food is enabled you will most likely heal enough HP and mana in order to continue hunting.

I added a Troll script as well. It will hunt Trolls till level 18/20 and then moves on to the Rotworm cave where it will eventually hunt Carrion Worms as well. It will start with an exp/h of about 5000 and will increase until it caps at 15000. It makes some nice Gold in the progress.

Higher level characters hunting this cave might consider deleting:
setLoot C0 0C (Sword)
setLoot D6 0C (Mace)
since they will be able to make more rounds per trip and therefore need more capacity for looted gold, dirt and fangs instead of the quite heavy maces and swords. This will eventually be more gold rewarding. Of course it's recommended to wear some decent gear so you can take some hits.

Enjoy!

Afwasmiddel


Troubleshooting:
- You die before finishing a waypoint because you ran out of spears or because you couldn't replenish your health enough: this can happen when facing big waves of rotworms and is more likely to happen at low (skill)levels or when carrying poor gear. I have my own conditionals that minimize the risk of dying because it ignores all creeps when you are out of potions or spears. I uploaded this conditional in a later post.
- You won't be able to replenish your spears and/or potions because the loot won't cover the new costs: this is more likely to happen when you loot lots of maces and swords and almost no dirt or money and you have no gold on your bank account. In this case you should consider hunting trolls a little longer to get some gold and levels. Better gear also helps.
- Your character gets stuck at a waypoint: this happens if the new waypoint is somehow blocked like the waypoint at the bank, luckily it usually recovers by itself whenever the way is cleared. However there were some moments where the script seemed to get stuck and didn't recover until I clicked somewhere else. This is certain to happen if you don't bring a rope and shovel with you.
- The bot will leave to the safezone and log out: the rotworms are not in your safelist, some douchebag PK'er tried to kill you or you started the script too close to the safezone. Start it at the weapon shop or at the cave entrances.
- The script will endlessly withdraw gold for potions and buy them. This is something you should check well. It's caused by a closed backpack. If your backpack isn't open the bot won't recognize the potions that are present in your backpack. Causing it to buy potions over and over again.

It is a script of the eastern southest Ghostland cave, it can be activated in the exit of the cave or in the cave. Must have a rope and a shovel.
It script begin in the entrance, killing the slime and another week monsters, later it go to the cave killing skeletons, ghouls, skeleton warriors, bats and poison spiders, getting 12k exp/h with level 15 and 16k exp/h with level 25-30, and very profitable for knights free account.
Only need 20 potions, plate set, and serpent/spike/crimson sword and a rope, the unique probles is you must have food because undead monsters don't give it.

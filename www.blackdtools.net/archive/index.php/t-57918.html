I made a dworc-script for my druid.

Go to this (http://tibia.wikia.com/wiki/Mapper?coords=127.140,128.130,7,3,1,1) cave. The script hunt all dworcs at the first floor.

It will loot tribal masks, voodo dolls, bast skirts and gold.


setFriendly
setMeleeKill centipede
setMeleeKill dworc fleshhunter
setMeleeKill dworc venomsniper
setMeleeKill dworc voodoomaster
setPriority dworc voodoomaster:+1
setSpellKill dworc voodoomaster,exori frigo,3
setBot OldLootMode=1
setLootDistance 3
setLoot D7 0B
setLoot BA 0B
setLoot 4B 0D
setLoot E8 0D

As long as you have some kind of rope or shovel the bot will automatically find it's way. Easiest way is to put a waypoint before and one after the level change, as in one adjecent to the hole and one at the rope spot.
But at most spawns where a creep spawns right under the hole the bot will kill all creeps in sight and then move back to the rope spot position before continuing at that level, looking very bot-like. One thing that might work is to put the waypoint a little bit in the direction you're heading efter changing level, just maybe 3-4 sqm and it doesn't look as bad. :p
You need to have all lootbackpacks open before starting, but don't minimize them. Looks odd if you loot into minimized backpacks. :p

http://i2.photobucket.com/albums/y9/alucarr/bptutorial/trainer881.jpg

(This require version 8.83 or higher to work properly)

1. Decide when you should stop attacking a monk (default is at 50 % hp)
2. Enable checkbox 7 (stop attacking target until regen)
3. Go to cavebot and load monktrainer.txt
4. Enable cavebot

IMPORTANT NOTE: on the latest versions you also need to check in "Enable Trainer"

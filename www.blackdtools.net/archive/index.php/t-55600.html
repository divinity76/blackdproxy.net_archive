Advices and minimum skills:

The real profit here is getting the 300 tarantulas from the task to kill Hide (wich give an average of 13k of loot)

21~27 K/ h, you will normally get it alone because this are not the 3 most famous tarantula's cave

50 mp and 15 hp are more than will need, but its better to avoid idiot risks

DON'T FORGET to bring food with you! Tarantulas don't drop any

I run it with K-set, Medusa Shield, Bright Sword, Leather Boots and Platinum Amulet (skills 68/68 now)

Enter in fourth entrance hole of the Tarantula's Cave and active it on your cave bot The 4 here: http://tibia.wikia.com/wiki/Tiquanda_Tarantula_Caves

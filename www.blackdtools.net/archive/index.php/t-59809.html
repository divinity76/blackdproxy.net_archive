New Christmas contest!
I need some nice pictures/photos for my giftcards so you can now submit your art for that purpose and you might win up to 1000k.

Reward:
First prize: 1000k
Second prize: 500k
Third prize: 500k
Note: Winners choose server. Reward can be gold of any standard open pvp or optional pvp server.

How to decide winners:
- I will only pick 1 valid picture/photo of each user who posted here before 21/Dic/2012
- If there are more than 10 users then I only will pick the best 10 for that poll according my personal criteria.
- Finally every registered forum user will be able to vote in the poll until 24/Dic/2012
- The ranking for the prizes will depend on the final poll results. User with more votes will get first prize, etc.
- The day 25/Dic/2012 I will send an email to the winners requesting char name/server so I can deliver the reward there.

Requirements of the submitted art:
- It can be a picture or a photo.
- Width should be exactly 851 pixels
- Height should be exactly 315 pixels
- If you need to resize your art please keep the aspect ratio! It should not look stretched. Better cut some borders instead.
- The format should be JPG
- It should try to evoke positive feelings.
- It should not be offensive for anyone.
- You should be the legal owner of that picture/photo
- You should allow me to use it as a background for the text of my gift cards.
- You should allow me to use it at my page at facebook.

I hope you like this new christmas contest!

Maybe lower the time needed for healing? If you set it to heal right when you get hit then the healing hotkey will be spammed a lot, taking priority over your walking, which can cause your char to stop moving sometimes.
Here's what I'm using:
Go to Settings, then HP and Mana:
1. Define delay between low status nad recharge - 200 = it means 0.2 second before it starts to heal you if needed
2. Define delay between recharges - 250 = it means 4x healing per second
3. Define percent of delay time randomized - 15 = it makes all above 15% random so it looks more like human

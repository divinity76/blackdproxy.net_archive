Perfect Script to upgrade your distance skill and get some exp.
Recommended for 8+ lvl with full Rookgaard set.

Get some:
http://www.tibia-wiki.net/images/Platinum_Coin.gif - Cash for spears.
http://www.tibia-wiki.net/images/Small_Health_Potion_Nap%C3%B3j.gif - Potions to heal yourself.

You will meet:
Wolves
Bugs
Spiders
Snakes

http://www.tibia-wiki.net/images/Spear.gif
Script will buy spears from Obi and hunt around the city.

Experience:
1,2k-2,2k/hour

Thanks to Malmis for script refiller.

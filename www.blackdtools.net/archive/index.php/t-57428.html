Client version?
1. change blackD in advanced tab to the correct version.
2. Make sure you use the windows version of Tibia and not the Flash client.
3. Change the ip on your Tibia client with your ip-changer
4. Go in proxy tab, "forward to OT server", insert your OT ip there and the correct port
5. Uncheck and check http://i40.tinypic.com/zjt0r8.png
6. Now login

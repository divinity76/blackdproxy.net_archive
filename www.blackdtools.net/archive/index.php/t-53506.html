Cooldow is the new name for exhausted. You know, when you cast a spell you have to wait a few seconds before cast another? It is the same thing but with a different name.
You might wonder, why they were implemented? Well, after the new update, spells like beams, ultimates, and some exori's will have 4s, 8s, 50s or even 60 seconds delay. So they had to create a new system to show user when is he going to be able to cast them again.
Basically, if you see the icons picture above, those icons are only to show you the exhaustion time. They cannot be clicked like elfbot nor even change their position on screen. :p

Easy.


onGMpause
SetMaxAttackTimeMs 2000000
setFriendly
setNoFollow
setMeleeKill Slime
gotoScriptLine 0

Load this on cavebot but does not activate.

Take a slime mother, hit loose and stop. Go to the trainer on BlackDProxy, press the button at the bottom right that says "Get ID of last Attacked" and select the option above that says "Avoid attacking the monster with this ID".

Now enable cavebot.

Greetings!

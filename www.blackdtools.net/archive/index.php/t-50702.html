Basically, lets just list sh*t we remember and see if we can build some interest. I'll Start:

-Orc Bridge :) eventually turned into dwarf bridge, had 1 or 2 zerks at it and maybe some warriors. only thing north of it at the time was carlin and a few dragons i believe. Orc fort may have also been in the game.

-Trapping the giant spider in cyc mnt by logging scout chars and having it spawn poison spiders :). once it was trapped, just log another char, pop up the hole, and shoot an energy field and 1 shot of an hmm at it :).

-Also with giant spiders (moving diagnol through trees in poh and killing with low lv pallies), or firebomb trapping them, had to hit GS on center field tho :P

-Tree trap pking by shooting a firefield when they were in diagnol trees. Also macro pushing PK'ing, Orio was the sh*t at this.

-One of the coolest weapons in the game which was removed (maybed replaced with the carlin sword?) it was a sword weapon (the three bladed sword) referred to as the three blader.

-When there was just "tibia", no other worlds until the introduction of Nova and Premia. There was no existence of premium accounts either :P

-Screen glitches (something we all don't miss) if you accidently pushed an arrow key too quickly following using stairs, a ladder/ramp, the screen would become totally f**cked.

-Old energy bombs that were actually sweet. Fillind damn near the entire screen with energy fields.

Well, I just wanted to try to get this started, and don't wanna make too long of a post that will drive people away, but i'll continue to add to this lil list, maybe this could be fun.

Here is a new script I slapped together that will run Torts and Blood crabs at the Tort cave in PH.
I have it setup to use exori con on blood crabs for a paladin, but if you wish you can remove that line.


#Made By Sohji444
#Port Hope Torts and Deposit
#Start at Bank in Port Hope
#Cave will Run all floors
setBot OldLootMode=1
setloot D7 0B
setloot D2 0B
setloot 0B 17
setloot 27 28
setMeleeKill cave rat
setMeleeKill snake
setMeleeKill slime
setMeleeKill crab
setMeleeKill crocodile
setMeleeKill tortoise
setMeleeKill carrion worm
setMeleeKill blood crab
setPriority tortoise:+1
setPriority blood crab:+2
setSpellKill blood crab,exori con,1
:Walk to Cave
move 32632,32740,6
move 32625,32748,6
move 32625,32750,7
move 32615,32717,7
move 32612,32703,7
waitX 3
useItem 32613,32703,7

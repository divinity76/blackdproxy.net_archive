Entrance to MoLS behind the level gates (with high enough skills you can train on a monk thats not a summon).
Facc desert (deep underground where noone but a lost noob will wander; summons and slimes, bring food, lots of it, ive heard stories of people leaving bots there for weeks at time).
At one point i used to macro train on summon in the cave in thais with bandits (back when there was only 3 bats spawning there).
Another place (altho known and I always check it when I go PK) is a small island located between folda and senja. You can reach it via the cave at folda.

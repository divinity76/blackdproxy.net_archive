Open the Trainer Window of Blackd Proxy
1)Choice the character
2)Mark the sqm where the loot are in the floor
3)Put id 00 00 to pick up all kind of items
4)Mark to pick it from floor to openned backpacks
5)The amount of items to pick up by turn
6)Open the bag/backpack for loot destination
7)Enable trainer to start pickup the loot to that openned backpacks

4.Cant see those damn stalkers? no problem! this option will turn them into statues enabling you to be able to cast runes at them in your battle window!

5.Change light intensity to: this will change the amount of light % on your screen dont use torch's use this!

6. AutoRuneheal if hp under X% this is most commenly used on all programs it makes it so when your health drops under X% it will heal you! this will use IH if your magic level isnt high enough (and if you have them on you ;) )

7.Auto Exura Vita, if hp under X% same with 6 but it will cast a spell to heal you "exura vita" great for mages and paladins!

8. This option lets you get remote oders, and send them aswell! great for wars! and combo's (ex fireSDnow:Jon)

9. Dont want to get remote orders from everyone? no problem put the selected name into this box to only accept orders from him/her, leave blank to have no leader!

10.This will open a new window that shows your actual map its very useful to find people and other things!

11.Backpacks! this option will load your backpacks and all items in it and check for items (ex. <d7 0b> you have 3 items found in your backpack)

12. show true map, this is definetly a VERY powerful map, it shows monsters even outside of your map under you and above you! although if on floor 7 it will not show below you!

13.Update now! this will update current position on all maps!

14.Ontop, check this to keep maps ontop!

15.auto update:this will update your current position every once and a while
update each x ms <__>:here you can chose when to update!
full auto update (slow!):this will update constantly but eat lots of cpu!

16.this is a list of ALL ingame commands!

17. This is how you will use uhs Classic mode requires a backpack to be open but takes no waiste!
Default option doesnt need open bps and has little % of waiste
and war mode has a very large amount of waiste!

18. If activated, on certain commands it will change your screen a diferent color to let you no just incase what youre doing!

19.Show exp in title window this will show selected in 25

20.blackd gratz when level, this will have a message saying gratz just incase you have no friends you should mark this! :p

21.auto relog at kick or serversave and open X bps, this is self explanatory and when enabled it will do exactly that, ofcourse you can set the amount of bps desired!

22.GM messages trigger specail events and pauses, when enabled if the bot sees a gm message it will pause all automatic functions!

23.this option will not let the bot shoot damage runes if your hp is below % for auto heal! this is recomended!

24. this is what determines to be said at the "exiva exp" message! keep this default!

25.this determines what it says in the tibia title bar put whatever you want (ex. Webber wuz here ya'll)

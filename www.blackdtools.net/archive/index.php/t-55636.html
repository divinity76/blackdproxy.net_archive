Hi!



I've played wow on and off since it's vanilla release in Europe in 2005. Here are the current answers to your questions;

1. For gold, try www.itemgarden.com they are based in HK and I have bought over 400k gold with them over the years

For characters try mmo-bay.net


2. Bots, well to be honest they are almost all detectable. The WoW client is extremely clever in detecting. I know several people who have used pirox and the other main "bot" program wow glider has been destroyed as blizzard entertainment took the creator to court in a high profile law suit.

3. Addons - you can literally customise your client anyway you want - it's not illegal, checkout www.curse-gaming.com and browse through the many thousands of ui mods and game addons people have coded.

4. Gold prices. These fluctuate hugely from server to server and faction to faction. Each server is it's on economy-literally. You can usually check how much gold is being farmed by checking prices on a site such as itemgarden. But beware, farming gold is not like tibia, prices can and do yoyo, one week 50,000 gold maybe worth $180USD and the week after gold sellers could have a surplus in supply and it could drop to $50.

Hope this helps you out. Let me know if you have any more questions.

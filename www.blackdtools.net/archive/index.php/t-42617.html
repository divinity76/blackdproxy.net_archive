Greetings. I haven't seen a script like this on the forums, it's very simple tho. It lets you train your skills if you're just too bad for slimes or can't get a ghoul for yourself. Just stand in a dwarf cave with training weapon and start it (the dwarves must be able to come to you after spawning without your movement). Using old loot mode because new bodies often covered the old one, you can edit if if you don't like it. I changed max attack time because it often started to attack another dwarf which was annoying. I used it on my knight today, works perfect. Please post comments!
P.S. loots gold, meat, mushrooms and iron ores (just in case :D)


onGMpause
SetMaxAttackTimeMs 2000000
setBot OldLootMode=1
setFriendly
setNoFollow
setMeleeKill poison spider
setMeleeKill dwarf
setMeleeKill rotworm
setLoot F8 16
setLoot D7 0B
setLoot F9 0D
setLoot 8B 0E
gotoScriptLine 0

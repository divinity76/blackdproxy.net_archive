This script is for Banuta, it will go around the outside and will go up some towers.

The option setBot LootAll=1 is enabled for better security and all the setLoot ID's aren't collected.

Looting:

- Gold (D7 0B)
- Banana Sash (F7 2C)
- Health Potion (0A 01)
- Kongra's Shoulderpad (CF 2C)
- Small Stone (F5 06)
- Plate Armor (1D 0D)
- Banana Staff (14 0D)
- Wand of Decay (00 0C)
- ...

Player killing

Players will be marked with a red skull for 7 days when they reach:

3+ unmarked characters in one day (24 hours) or
5+ unmarked characters in one week (7 days).
Note that every time one of these limits is reached again the red skull mark is reset to 7 days.

Players will be marked with a black skull for 15 days when they reach:

5+ unmarked characters in one day (24 hours) or
8+ unmarked characters in one week (7 days).
Red skulled players:

Drop all items when they die unless wearing a broken amulet.
Black skulled players:

Drop all items when they die unless wearing a broken amulet.

As you all know, the virus is a false positive. Nothing to worry about. But due to come bad anti-virus programs, it is not letting Blackd open. I've seen many threads about Blackd not being able to open.
*FOR AVG USERS ONLY*
I use the free edition of AVG 8.0 as my anti-virus and today i got the problem (Trojan Horse). I fixed it though and ill tell you how. All you need to do is make Blackd an exception on resident shield. If you dont know how to do that here is how:
1- Open AVG, and click Resident Shield.
2- Go to Manage Exceptions.
3- Now, go to Add Path and find the path to your blackd.( For Example, The path I used was C:\Program Files\Blackd Tools\Blackd Proxy\ ) This will be the case for most people. I suggest you use that path.
3- Then click Ok.
4- IMPORTANT CLICK SAVE CHANGES!
5- It should now bring you back to the Main page (Overview)

And there you go.

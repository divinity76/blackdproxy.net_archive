Hello.

I made very simply script for new paladins , hunt trolls , buy spears and deposit gold .

2.5k exp/h .

Set autorefill in trainer !

Need a lot of cap , you should trow shovel , rope etc .

Have funn :)



#By Wiotki
setLootOn
setFollow
SetMaxAttackTimeMs 90000
setChaoticMovesOFF
setLoot D7 0B
setLoot CD 0C
setMeleeKill troll
:exp
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32116,31977,11
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32099,31978,11
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32100,31954,11
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32100,31933,11
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32121,31941,11
IfTrue ($countitems:CD 0C$#number<=#6) Goto $nlineoflabel:spear$
move 32118,31965,11
gotoScriptLine 0
:spear
move 32116,31977,11
move 32116,31975,10
move 32118,31978,10
move 32118,31976,9
move 32116,31978,9
move 32116,31976,8
move 32112,31987,8
waitX 1
fastExiva > 0A 00 82 70 7D F4 7C 08 F2 1A 01 01
move 32112,31992,8
move 32112,31990,7
move 32098,31989,7
sayMessage hi
waitX 2
waitX 2
sayInTrade deposit all
waitX 2
sayInTrade yes
waitX 2
fastExiva _spearneed = $numericalexp:{$mycap$}/2000$
fastExiva _spearcount = $numericalexp:{$_spearneed$}-1$
fastExiva _spearcost = $numericalexp:{$_spearcount$}*10$
sayInTrade withdraw $_spearcost$
waitX 2
sayInTrade yes
waitX 2
sayInTrade trade
waitX 2
fastExiva > 07 00 7A CD 0C 00 $numbertohex2:{$_spearcount$}$ 00

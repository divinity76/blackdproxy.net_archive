Jim and Huck are working together, so the average time to make a raft if they where only one person would be:

6 + 5 / 2 = 5,5

But they are two guys so: 5,5 / 2 = 2,75 average.

Then you do the proportion:

2,75 = 100%
3,5 = x

x = 100*3,5/2,75
x = 127,27 %

So 27,27% over the time they where supposed to finish. The nearest whole percent is 27%.

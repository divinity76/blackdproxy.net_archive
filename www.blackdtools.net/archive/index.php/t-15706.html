Rook Sewer Rats, Made By Odiria.



What you need:

As an suggestion i would do like this before i started this script:

1.
<Two things to choose>
a) Go killing some deers and rabbits, then sell the food you get there to willy.
b) Go kill some rats, then take the rats that you killed, then sell them.

2. Do one of these choises until you got around 75 gp ~~

3. When you got enough cash, go to obi and buy this ;
Leather armor
Leather legs
Backpack
Short sword

4. Now you should be equipped enough. Just go down the sewer and start the cavebot.


Features (Lol?):
Killing rats until level 8, xD
Walking to oracle at level 8.



I made this script with some help on looking at Malmis script called, "Rook to main v2.0", So thank you malmis :P.


(If you are planning to use this when you are asleep, i would suggest to change the "onGMpause" to "onGMcloseConnection".)

It is a simple script of goblins in Femur Hills, to collect small stones.
You only need a rope and a plate set for no waste. I tested it on a character level 25 in 8.71,
I was getting 4.5k exp/h and 25 small stones+300 gp/h being 1k gps per hour for a level 25 free account.



setLootOn
setBot OldLootMode=1
setLoot D7 0B
setLoot 13 2D
setLoot F5 06
setMeleeKill bug
setMeleeKill Goblin Assassin
setPriority goblin assassin:+3
setMeleeKill Goblin Scavenger
setPriority goblin scavenger:+2
setMeleeKill rat
setMeleeKill Poacher
setMeleeKill wolf
setMeleeKill bat
setMeleeKill goblin
setPriority goblin:+1
setMeleeKill pig

Create a character and login, then run the script.

DO NOT TOUCH THE EQ/BACKPACK OF YOUR CHARACTER UNTIL IT HAS BOUGHT EQ!

This script's also for free accounts!

What it does:
- Does tutorial
- Hunts for food and sells it, until it has enough money for equipment
- Buys decent eq
- Goes to Aldee rats (Free account version) or Premium rats (Premium account version)
- Deposits money and logs out some exp before lvl 9.

I've also uploaded a script which goes straight to rook temple and so skipping the tutorial. Just start the script after you logged in.

Only use the premium script on a premium account!

Versions

V1.0
V1.1 - Removed the part where it went to bugs at a specific level. It's too dangerous.
V2.0 - It will buy a backpack from now on. Also some other small improvements. Also uploaded the premium account version of the script!
V2.1 - Fixed the cockroach part. Also disabled the messages you get from the bot to avoid banishments.
V2.2 - Fixed the tranch part, and deleted waypoints in the cockroachcave. You won't be stuck there anymore. Note that you're character will keep killing cockroaches until there aren't any at your screen. I can't solve this.


UNCHECK ALLOW REPOSITION AT START!

I used small parts of Darky's script for this but redone & improved parts myself too. This script is fully working on tibia 8.4!

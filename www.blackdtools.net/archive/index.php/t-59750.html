Scrit is meant for PALADINS, sorry i didn't mention on the title.

This is an adaptation from Zhinu's script (http://www.blackdtools.net/showthread.php?59677-Larvas-Center-for-paladin-100) for lavas.

It goes to -2 and hunt the scarabs and larvas.
-level 25+ with decent equip.
-starts at the city's depot.
-loots emeralds, sapphires, scarab shells, scarab coins and gold.
Makes around 25k exp/h
Goes to the city to deposit your gold and buys ROYAL SPEARS.

I recommend you go to the ? section of the proxy and set your condition to $statusbit:8$ #number=# 1 and say exana pox.
Auto healing is also recommended (i put my exura at 75%and health and pot at 25%)

The bot won't buy potions so it's about 90% afk (check it sometimes to see if it's alright).

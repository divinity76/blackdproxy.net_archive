I call this, 10 easy steps by BlackD Proxy software ^_^:

1.) Open BlackD Proxy
2.) Open Trainer
3.) Choose desirable character
4.) Choose desirable sqm
5.) Set ITEM ID to '00 00' (even says it in the bot itself: "write 00 00 to pick any item")
6.) Set destination of items to 'Any Backpack'
7.) Set max items you can carry to something like: '999999'
8.) Open desirable backpacks where you want your items to fly in (even if the backpacks are on the floor)
9.) Enable Trainer
10.) Watch your items fly into opened backpacks.

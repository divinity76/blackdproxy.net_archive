Hum, why wouldn't use just HP&MANA section of the bot?

Example: It will heal you with exura ico when your HP drops under 80% of your all HP and will drink a mana potion when your MANA drops under 50% of your all MANA.

http://i.imgur.com/5w5eV.png

And for 'mana burning', so your mana will never be full you can use this conditional event:


$mymana$
#number>=#
200
0
1000
0
exura ico

200 = mana amount

OR


$mymanapercent$
#number>#
95
0
1000
0
exura ico

95 = percentage of your mana

EDIT: "edit: also is there a way to make blackd make it so i make sure i use enough mana before using a mana pot to heal with my spells? i dont want to waste mana from potions "

Just set to drink a mana potion at lower percentage, but enough so you'll have enough mana for 'exura ico' or any other spell.

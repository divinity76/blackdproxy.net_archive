On cavebot window of Blackd you will find, behind 创Change autoheal at pk创 a new option with HP for exori vis.

It means, the bot will keep spamming vis, if the creature is in front of you and you got mana, if you COND is on 80% and your setting on 创HP for exori vis创 is at 70% you will heal at 70 and not at 80. Because the bot will be spamming vis till creature dies.

So make sure if you want to heal at 80% , to have the HP for exori vis创 at 80% , if not, it will not stop vising.

On certain creatures like ancient scarabs, dragon lords, behemoths is recomended to make very sure about all this.

Just for your own wellfare.

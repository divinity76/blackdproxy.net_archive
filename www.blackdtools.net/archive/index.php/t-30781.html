Hi!

Kazordoon is a dwarven underground settlement with the official status of Empire located inside a big mountain called "The Big Old One". Obviously its inhabited with all the classes of Dwarves, some Dwarves are pacific persons at stores but other dwarves are aggressive and attack anyone that get into their big city. It also leads entrance to the dwarven prison of Dwacatra. Good for all levels because of Mines are good for skilling, getting level. You can make many lootbags getting 1k/per 1000oz, it has a mailbox (only for the ones who have completed The Postman Missions Quest.) In update 8.2 (2.07.2008) Kazordoon was remapped and has new features like a own Ore wagon transport system, you need to buy a ticket for 250 gold coins (it is useful just for 1 week) and go into a wagon, you will be transported to your selected destination. Destinations are: Depot, Entrance, Temple, Shops and The Steamship. New Walls and other furniture were added too, New "Quest" was implemented.

Kazordoon comes of Khazad-dûm, which means "Mine of the Dwarves" in accordance with the Khuzdul, the language of the Dwarves in the universe of Lord of the Rings.

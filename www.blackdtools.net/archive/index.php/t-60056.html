BUT!!! I've got a little trick you can do, if you ever miss a screenshot. I've already used it when I've missed a screeshot and will probably use it from now on when I'll be hunting AFK missing all those advances.

exiva < B4 12 $hex-tibiastr:TEXT HERE$

Use this command line. Just say it in local chat. Obviously edit 'TEXT HERE'.

Just an example: exiva < B4 12 $hex-tibiastr:You advanced from Level 998 to Level 999.$

That would set white text (You advanced from Level 998 to Level 999.) in the middle of your Tibia screen, just like at the times you're advancing in skills or such.

1.This shows what character the cavebot is botting with the script chosen
2.This shows the script of your choice and all of its commands
3.This shows how long (ms) it will pause before going onto next waypoint or contineing on, 1000 ms is equal to 1 second!
4.To use this Fill in the space with the monster you want to kill, and ofcourse get rid of "larva"
5.This chooses what you want to use, but you will have to use item "ID" a step to step guide is located here: http://www.blackdtools.net/showthread.php?p=1059#post1059
6.If you are afraid of something being luired on you and you have a backpack of HMM's set this option and fill in the blank with whatever monster you feel indangered of.
7.If put into your script it will close on sight of a GM although it is not recommended since it usually results in a ban!
8.OntrapGiveAlarm this command is self explanatory, if your trapped and cant move it will sound the alarm (alarm can be tested by saying "exiva testsound" in game window)
9.If you are being pk'ed or being attacked by a monster not on your safelist, it will goto the line demmanded to goto!
10.setAny will kill all monsters scripted, its default.
11.setFriendly will only attack monsters not being attacked/attacking other people on screen!
12.setFollow will follow the monster if it runs, or if your to far away, This is default.
13.ongGMpause will pause ALL cheats/bots this is VERY HIGHLY recomended it is also recomended to always be near computer to hear the alarm just incase!
14.onPlayerpause- this will pause all cheats/bots besides auto-uh, when a player comes on your screen!
15.setVeryFriendly will only attack monsters attacking you, if a player is on your screen
16.setLooton this will loot all items scripted, This is default.
17.setLootoff this will stop looting all monsters corpses, if you want to make experience just a little faster this would help (tiny bit!:p)
18.setNofollow this will not follow any monster if it is running or to far away from you, this is not default! and recomended for training scripts:)

Well, as far as i know: 1. If the following condition is true, jump to (5) line; 2. The thing it will check and see wether to jump to (5) line or not (like current mana, life, exp, etc); 3. The relation between the thing you specified at (2) with the thing you will specify at (4), like, if the thing1 is >,< or = the thing2; 4. The comparation you are making with thing1; 5. If the statement is true, will jump to this line. For example: IfTrue($mymana$#number>#400)Goto 63; That means if your mana is higher than 400 it will jump to line 63. 8. Just a comment at the script (it wont be read by the program), so you can leave your credits or take notes about some part of the script so you know what it means quickly. 10. Works like a name for a part of the script, so if u want like to jump to it, you will just specify the labelname and even if u exchange its place it will still read it right.
